Consumer Energy Saving Reward Service

Service

Description: Energy Saving rewarding

Goal of the service
Based on the actual P1 data the energy saving is determined for the gas and electricity consumption. The actual P1 data is referenced to the historical consumption corrected with actual temperature and the appropriate usage category. Energy saving is rewarded with energy coins. P4 data provided by the network operator is used for validation of the P1 data for determining the final energy saving. The selected service provider will read the data every 10 seconds. The user is in control to start and stop the subscription. The provided P1 data is not used for other purposes.
